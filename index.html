WHO WE ARE

Artistic Souls

THE MAGAZINE OF CONTEMPORARY ART is THE MAGAZINE OF CONTEMPORARY ART is an online publication covering visual arts from around the world. It is designed to assume an international platform where artists can be connected directly with art professionals, collectors, and art galleries, bringing their art to a worldwide viewership of over three billion internet users.

By showcasing your artwork in a friendly environment that encourages and inspires artistic growth, we strive to help you find your artistic voice. THE MAGAZINE OF CONTEMPORARY ART has a mission to create a safe, communal space for all kinds of artists – promising beginners to seasoned professionals – to come together and express their creative sides. We believe in unlocking the creative side within each and every one and offer buyers a wide range of media, styles, and talents to choose from. So, whether you’re a professional looking for new inspiration, a brand new student hoping to learn a thing or two about art, or simply interested in picking up a new hobby, THE MAGAZINE OF CONTEMPORARY ART is here to welcome you into the centerfold.
